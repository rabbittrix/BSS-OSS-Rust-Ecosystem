## Frequently Asked Questions

### Can I use TMF APIs for free?

- **Non-commercial use:** Yes, free to use
- **Commercial use:** Requires authorization and donation
- **Open source projects:** Generally permitted, contact for clarification

### What is considered "commercial use"?

Commercial use includes:

- Using the software in a commercial product or service
- Using the software to provide services to paying customers
- Using the software in a for-profit organization
- Redistributing the software as part of a commercial offering

### How do I get authorization for TMF APIs?

Contact Roberto de Souza <rabbittrix@hotmail.com> with:

- Your use case
- Company information
- Intended deployment
- Donation commitment

### What are the license fees for commercial components?

License fees are determined based on:

- Number of users/endpoints
- Deployment environment
- Support requirements
- Customization needs

Contact us for a custom quote.

### Can I contribute code?

Yes! Contributions are welcome. See [CONTRIBUTING.md](CONTRIBUTING.md) for guidelines. Contributors retain copyright to their contributions but grant the project license to use them.

### What about modifications?

- **TMF APIs:** You may modify under Apache 2.0 terms, but commercial use still requires authorization
- **Commercial Components:** Modifications are subject to license terms - contact for details
